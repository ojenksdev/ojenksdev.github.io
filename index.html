About Me

I'm a Web Developer, Python Programmer & IT Professional from the D.C Metropolitan area (Now living in Dallas, TX). I have been working as an IT contractor and freelance web developer for nearly 10 years now, with companies ranging from start-ups to major corporations. Some of the languages I enjoy working with include Python, HTML, CSS & JavaScript. In addition, I regularly build websites in some of the most popular platforms & frameworks including Wordpress, Squarespace, Shopify & Bootstrap. I also compose music in my spare time.


I'm a Crypto asset and Blockchain tech enthusiast and blog about both from time to time. I also have a python blog dedicated to the language and the pitfalls & positives of self-learning a programming language.


In 2016 I started my own website management company called Webmaster On Site, The focus being website maintenance for small businesses. It took up quite a bit of my time, so now I offer similar services as a freelancer from here. Always looking to connect with new interesting people!
